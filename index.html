How to Play

Bugs On A Rug is a matching game where players try and clear the bugs off of the rug!
On your turn click the "Click for Color" button and match it to the color of a bug on the rug to clear it.
If you can't make a match you get a stink bug, collect 3 stink bugs and you lose!
Good luck and have fun!
